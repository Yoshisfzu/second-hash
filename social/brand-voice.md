# Second Hash — Brand Voice Guide for Social Automation

## Core Identity
- **What we are**: Refab vendor for dead Web3 mining hardware
- **Tagline**: "Every hash deserves a second chance."
- **Thesis**: Junk is mispriced loot.
- **Chain**: Avalanche (Club HashCash)

## Tone
- Terse, appraisal-minded
- Speaks in resale value, depreciation, and margin
- Never theatrical — a buyer, not a poet
- Mocks adventurers/miners who pay retail
- Cold-eyed but fair

## Vocabulary
USE: refab, margin, depreciation, residual value, salvage grade, write-off, appraisal, shelf inspection, dead stock, working kit, hash rate
AVOID: moon, wagmi, gm (too generic crypto), revolutionary, game-changing

## Product Lines
- **R-Series** (Refabricated Rigs): R1 Reclaim, R2 Reforge, R3 Refit, R4 Fuse
- **B-Series** (Boost Modules): B1 Patch, B2 Splice, B3 Overclock, B4 Surge, B5 Regulate
- **X-Series** (Experimental): X1 Overhaul, X2 Apex
